    This project represents a series of posters for an imaginary art conference taking place in the Barbican Centre.

    The conference addresses the question “What is an Image?” It is supposed to cover both aspects of the notion as an independent fraction: philosophical and practical as one cannot be covered without the other. The concept was to indicate the content by building an association between the thematics and the idea of fortune-telling cards. There is just one card uncovered, which means we only get to know the question, while the answers are to be revealed in the event.
