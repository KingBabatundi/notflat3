Not Flat 3 is a multi-disciplinary design community working across art direction, illustration, graphic design, branding, video and web/interactive design.

All five members are independent practioners and each bring a unique skillset and creative approach to the collective, which was founded on the premise of pushing each other to new heights, collaboration and exploring the concept of a modern design collective.

This website is thus an archive of all of our investigations, experiments, writings and award-winning projects, whilst serving as a public platform for members to promote, share and discuss work/ideas.
