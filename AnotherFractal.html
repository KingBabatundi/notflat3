    The idea of the project was to address the topic of environmental change by combining both fine art and graphic design approaches.

    The outcome is a series of three-dimensional exhibits, representing the physical changes the Earth is constantly undergoing. The following images comprise various fractal patterns, which displayed at different scale and level, provide a visual reference to the repetitive nature of organic change.
